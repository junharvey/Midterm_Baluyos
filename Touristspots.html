Explore the Hidden Gems of Dapitan City

Discover the charm and beauty of Dapitan City through its historical sites, natural attractions, and vibrant culture. Plan your next adventure and immerse yourself in the wonders of this hidden gem in the Philippines.

The Rizal Shrine

The Rizal Shrine is a reproduction of the original two-story, Spanish-colonial style house in Calamba, Laguna where José Rizal was born on June 19, 1861.[1] Rizal is regarded as one of the greatest national heroes of the Philippines.

Rizal's Landing Place

Rizal Landing Site is one of the latest developed monuments in honor of the Philippines’ National Hero, Dr. Jose P. Rizal. Rizal Landing Site is situated in Sta. Cruz beach where Jose Rizal landed at 7:00 pm on July 17, 1892 to begin his expatriation life in Dapitan.

Gloria's Fantasyland

Gloria's Fantasyland is the only theme park in Visayas and Mindanao who assertively brings world class shows and entertainment to the public, who are goal-oriented to its target market. Gloria's Fantasyland decisively make choice quickly and confidently to deliver good service to clients.

St. James the Greater Parish

The parish that is now known as St. James the Greater Parish Church was established in 1631 by Fr. Pedro Gutierres, a Jesuit priest. The present church, which also goes by the names Dapitan Church and Santiago Mayor Parish Church, is a 5-minute walk from the foot of Ilihan Hill.

Canlucani Beach

The long stretch of white beach sand and the turquoise blue water of Canlucani beach are the sights to behold. However, during that time, it’s low tide so we need to walk for several minutes to be able to dip in the waters.

Ilihan Hill

Trees and vegetation make up most of the hill where you can see overlooking the sea and the city of Dapitan. It also serves as peaceful respite and /or romantic hideaway for some couples going up there.

Dakak Park and Beach Resort

Discover a hidden gem tucked in a lush paradise where the vast greenery meets the sand and sea. Located in the historical city of Dapitan in Zamboanga del norte, Dakak Resort and Properties is a destination that demands to be experienced. Embrace the simplicity of countryside living while you wander off the majestic work of nature.
